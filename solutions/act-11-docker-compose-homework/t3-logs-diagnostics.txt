Log Diagnostics Explanation:
* Aggregated Logs (docker compose logs): Better for understanding the overall health of the application and seeing the chronological sequence of events and interactions between different containers (e.g., seeing the web service connect to the database right after the database starts).
* Single-Service Logs (docker compose logs [service_name]): Better for targeted debugging when you know a specific component is failing (like tracing a Python error in the web app) without being distracted by the logs from healthy services.
